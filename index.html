Developing A Multi-Layered Cherenkov Detector

Advisor: Mr. Mir Faruk

2025-04-01

This paper introduces a low-cost, modular multi-layered Cherenkov detector that identifies particles by comparing photon yield ratios across different dielectric media, eliminating the need for ring imaging. Simulations and cosmic-ray tests validate the approach, with a proposed CERN beamline test to demonstrate accurate particle discrimination across 1–15 GeV/c in an accessible, reproducible design.
